Chicken fry

Description

OMG! this is crazy-crisp, spicy and super good! Yes, the Chicken Fry that you will never be bored of. The juicy chicken pieces blanketed in a mix of basic spices, then deep fried to crisp outer and succulent within.

Ingredients

  • 250 grams cubed boneless chicken
  • 1 - 1.5 tsp fresh ginger garlic paste
  • ½ tsp red chilli powder
  • ½ tsp black pepper powder
  • ¼ tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 - 2 tsp lime juice
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying

Steps to cook

  1. Clean the chicken pieces and place in a bowl
  2. Add all the spices, ginger garlic paste, salt, and lime juice to this
  3. Mix well until the chicken pieces are well coated. You can also mix the marinade ingredients first in the bowl and then add the chicken pieces and mix together.
  4. Cover and refrigerate for a few hours. You can also fry immediately if short on time.
  5. Heat oil for deep frying and gently add the chicken pieces to it without overcrowding them.
  6. Fry on medium heat stirring occasionally until the chicken pieces are golden brown.
  7. Take out one piece and test to see if the pieces are fully cooked.
  8. When done, drain and place on kitchen towels for the excess oil to get absorbed.
  9. Proceed with the remaining pieces until all done.
  10. Serve hot with rice, any curry, and a simple salad. It's best to consume chicken fry on the same day you prepare them
